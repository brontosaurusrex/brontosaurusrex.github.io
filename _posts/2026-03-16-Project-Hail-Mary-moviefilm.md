## Cons

The film repeatedly crosses the thin line between genuine feel-good moments and overly sentimental “Disney-style” feel-good. The “Am I smart?” moment—oh my god. The music tries too hard to force emotions that aren’t really there. The alien also makes some strange “cute” noises that feel a bit much.

## Pros

Visually beautiful. Ryan is mostly a good fit for the role. Rocky having five legs.
