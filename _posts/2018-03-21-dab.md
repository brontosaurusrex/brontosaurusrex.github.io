## DVB-T

### mpv

	# ? = country code
	w_scan -ft -c "?" -M > .config/mpv/channels.conf
	/usr/bin/mpv dvb://"CHANNEL NAME" --no-resume-playback -vf yadif=field
    
or to add many to 'playlist'

	... dvb://"CHAN 1" dvb://"CHAN 2" ...

### vlc

	vlc dvb://frequency=562000000 :program=2
