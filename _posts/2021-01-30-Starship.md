## Simpler config

In ~/.config/starship.toml

    [line_break]
    disabled = false
    
    [cmd_duration]
    min_time = 10000

In .zshrc

    # starship
    eval "$(starship init zsh)"
    RPROMPT="" # disable zsh right prompt
