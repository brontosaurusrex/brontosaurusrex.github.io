drop that into user bin

<pre>du -hca --max-depth=1 "$@" | sort -h</pre>

usage examples: 

duh .
  
duh ~/videos ~/downloads

Will return sub-directory and files with human readable sizes sorted, like;

`528K	./youtube-dl<br />
624K	./lib<br />
996K	./gisele.phar<br />
3.9M	./data.txt<br />
4.4M	./mtn<br />
13M	./ffmpeg_tmp<br />
18M	./mpv_stable<br />
19M	./mpv<br />
19M	./mpv.bak<br />
19M	./mpv_old<br />
21M	./ffmpeg_static<br />
21M	./ffprobe<br />
42M	./f265cli<br />
